About InstaJam

InstaJam was built by a musician who wanted one thing: to make it easier for artists to meet, collaborate, and create together. No more searching endlessly for bandmates, no more missed connections — just pure musical synergy at your fingertips.

His mission is simple: empower musicians of all backgrounds to find their sound, grow their craft, and connect with a vibrant creative community. Whether you're a seasoned performer or picking up your instrument for the first time, InstaJam is here to help you make something unforgettable.

Privacy Policy

InstaJam respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

We collect only the data necessary to provide core features, including:

  • Account authentication
  • Profile creation and discovery
  • Messaging and group chats
  • Event hosting and participation

Your data is never sold.

We may share limited information with trusted third-party services (such as Firebase) strictly to operate and secure the app. These services are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

You may request account deletion at any time. When an account is deleted, associated personal data is removed in accordance with our data retention policies.
